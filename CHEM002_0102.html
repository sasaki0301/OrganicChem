Organic vs Inorganic Compounds (Comparing Properties)

Organic and inorganic compounds differ primarily in their composition and structure. Organic compounds are primarily composed of carbon atoms bonded to hydrogen, oxygen, or nitrogen, while inorganic compounds typically do not contain carbon-hydrogen bonds.
